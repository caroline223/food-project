About Us

The Process

SET UP AN ACCOUNT

First, register an account with us. After setting up an account, you would be able to submit requests for pickup and have food delivered to you :) !


ORDERING YOUR FOOD

Next, search for food from your local restaurant of choice--so long as it is within our delivery area-- and our system will find the closest driver at the best cost to deliver to you.


CONFIRM YOUR REQUEST

Lastly, before you submit your request, you have the opportunity to make any changes or cancelations deemed necessary.



Delivery Options


CLASSIC

After selecting your food of choice from the restaurant of choice, your order would be delivered within 30 - 40 minutes upon receipt of that request. Delivery can either be contact or no contact.


PREMIUM

After selecting your food of choice from the restaurant of choice, your order would be delivered within 15 - 20 minutes upon receipt of that request. Also, you would get faster response times if an issue arise with your order. Furthermore, you earn reward points everytime you order from us. Delivery can either be contact or no contact.


ROYALTY

After selecting your food of choice from the restaurant of choice, your order would be delivered within 10 - 15 minutes upon receipt of that request. Also, you would get an immediate response if an issue arise with your order. Furthermore, you earn double reward points everytime you select this option. Delivery can either be contact or no contact.
